## Overview
Installing Matter AI's official Github App enables Notifications and Approval flows

## Steps

1. Visit https://app.matterai.dev/connectors and then click on Connect under Github.

<img
  className="block"
  src="/images/connectors/connectors.png"
  alt="Matter AI Connectors"
/>

2. On the Slack App installation page, select the organisation and click `Allow`:

<img
  className="block"
  src="/images/connectors/slack-app.png"
  alt="Matter AI Connectors"
/>


<Note>Once installed, Matter AI will fetch the list of channels and it's members. Matter AI does not have permission to read any Message, unless Gravity App is tagged in the same</Note>
